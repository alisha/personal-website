I'm Alisha Ukani

I'm a fourth-year CS PhD student at UC San Diego. My research focuses on Internet measurement and security/privacy, focusing on how technology interacts with society and governments. My advisor is Alex Snoeren.

I'm supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and the ARCS Foundation San Diego Fellowship. I also serve as the Co-President of UCSD's Graduate Women in Computing (GradWIC) group.

You can contact me at aukani@ucsd.edu.
