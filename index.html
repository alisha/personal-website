I'm Alisha Ukani

I'm a final-year CS PhD candidate at UC San Diego advised by Alex C. Snoeren. My research focuses on measuring and improving web privacy, with an emphasis on practical applications and emerging privacy concerns (e.g. AI browser agents and forged search warrants).

My PhD has been supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and an ARCS Foundation San Diego Fellowship. I also served as the Co-President of UCSD's Graduate Women in Computing (GradWIC) group.

You can contact me at aukani@ucsd.edu.
