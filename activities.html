Girls Who Code Weston (2014-Present)

Mentor (2014-Present)

Every week, I mentor our future programmers: middle school girls! I help them organize ideas and problem solve as they make games in Scratch.


Code for Boston (2014-Present)

I'm a dedicated member of Code for Boston, a Code for America organization that aims to better the lives of citizens in Boston.

I'm currently working on Ungentry, a map of urbanization and gentrification patterns in Boston. In addition to working on the UX, I'm also adding data on home mortgage requests.

When I first joined, I made a guide to help local animal shelters make Twitter bots that tweet about their adoptable pets.


Weston High School Science Team (2012-2016)

Co-Captain (2015-2016), Secretary (2014-2015)

Our Science Team participates in Science Olympiad, both in regional and state competitions. I've competed in rounds like Cell Biology, Designer Genes, Chem Lab, Chem Clue, Fossils, Forensics, Evolution, Picture This, Write It/Do It, and Fossils.

As a secretary, I took attendance at weekly meetings, sent email summaries of meetings, sent sureys for team members' round preferences, helped organize round assignments, organized study workshops, planning our first USABO competition, and organized fun things like secret psyche and t-shirts.

As next year's co-captain, I'm excited to continue my leadership by leading weekly meetings, organizing round assignments, organizing study sessions, and more.


Weston High School Math Team (2012-2016)

Weston High School Computer Science Team (2012-2016)

Our Computer Science Team is mostly a space to work on and show off cool projects! We also participate in the Providence College High School Programming Contest. I'm in the Silver Division.

Weston High School Principal's Advisory Council (2013-2015)
